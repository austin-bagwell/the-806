About

The 806 Coffee + Lounge

Established in 2007 in the Historic Route 66 District, we offer the best coffee, espresso, tea, vegetarian/vegan foodies, beers, and wines! We're proud to host an eclectic mix of local and traveling music acts, as well as Amarillo's longest-running Open Mic Night!
